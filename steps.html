What are some steps we can take to help?

  1. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Consume less by reducing your waste and reusing items when possible. Properly recycle materials to reduce landfill waste.
  2. Conserve Energy: Use energy-efficient appliances, turn off lights and electronics when not in use, and consider installing solar panels on your home.
  3. Plant Trees: Trees absorb carbon dioxide and provide oxygen. Support tree-planting initiatives in your community.
  4. Reduce Food Waste: Plan meals, use leftovers, and compost food scraps to minimize food waste.
With these simple little steps, you can save some money while doing your part to fight against climate change!
